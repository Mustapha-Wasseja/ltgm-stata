LTGM Stata Package -- Data Sources
====================================

The bundled country dataset (ltgm_country_data.dta) combines data from
the following sources. All data used under their respective open licenses.


1. Penn World Tables 11.0 (PWT)
   ------------------------------------------------------------------
   Citation:
     Feenstra, R.C., R. Inklaar and M.P. Timmer (2015).
     "The Next Generation of the Penn World Table."
     American Economic Review, 105(10), 3150-3182.

   Variables sourced:
     - TFP growth (multiple averaging periods: 20yr, 15yr, 10yr, 5yr)
     - Capital share / labour share
     - Depreciation rate
     - Capital-output ratio (K/Y)
     - Human capital growth

   URL: https://www.rug.nl/ggdc/productivity/pwt/
   License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)

   Notes:
     PWT 11.0 is the primary source. When PWT 11.0 produces implausibly
     low TFP values (0 <= g_tfp < 0.5%) due to capital stock methodology
     revisions, the package falls back to PWT 10.0 then PWT 9.0.
     Negative TFP values are preserved as genuine economic signals.


2. World Development Indicators (WDI), World Bank
   ------------------------------------------------------------------
   Citation:
     World Bank (2024). World Development Indicators.
     Washington, D.C.: The World Bank Group.

   Variables sourced:
     - GDP per capita (GNI Atlas method, current USD)
     - Gross fixed capital formation (% of GDP) -- investment rate
     - Poverty headcount ratio at $2.15/day (2017 PPP)
     - Poverty headcount ratio at $3.65/day (2017 PPP)
     - Poverty headcount ratio at $6.85/day (2017 PPP)
     - Gini coefficient
     - Labour force participation rate (total, male, female)
     - GDP growth rate (10-year average)

   URL: https://data.worldbank.org
   License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)


3. UN World Population Prospects 2024 (UNWPP)
   ------------------------------------------------------------------
   Citation:
     United Nations, Department of Economic and Social Affairs,
     Population Division (2024). World Population Prospects 2024.

   Variables sourced:
     - Population growth rate (medium variant, annual projections)
     - Working-age to total population ratio (15-64)

   URL: https://population.un.org/wpp/
   License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 IGO (CC BY 3.0 IGO)


4. IMF World Economic Outlook (WEO)
   ------------------------------------------------------------------
   Variables available in CSV (not primary source for defaults):
     - Investment rate (WEO forecast variant)

   URL: https://www.imf.org/en/Publications/WEO
   License: Subject to IMF terms of use


5. IMF Fiscal Affairs Department (FAD)
   ------------------------------------------------------------------
   Variables available in CSV (not primary source for defaults):
     - Capital-output ratio (FAD estimate)
     - Public investment share

   URL: https://www.imf.org/en/Research
   License: Subject to IMF terms of use


Reference year
------------------------------------
All cross-sectional indicators are referenced to 2022 (the most recent
year with broad coverage across PWT 11.0 and WDI). Population growth
projections use the UNWPP 2025 medium variant as the base-year value.
